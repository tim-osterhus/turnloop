Improve Corebound.

Treat it as the flagship game in the arcade. Pick the highest-leverage improvement to moment-to-moment play, clarity, progression, feel, or robustness. Favor work players can immediately feel over abstract cleanup, unless a reliability fix is required to keep the game improving safely.

Aim for one meaningful step forward, not a pile of scattered tweaks. Keep the game playable throughout. If a riskier idea would make the game brittle, cut it down to the strongest safe slice and ship that instead.
